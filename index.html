StudyForgeJS — Welcome!

What is StudyForgeJS?

StudyForgeJS is a browser-based study app that lets you upload, create, and study custom flashcard decks entirely in the browser — without the need of an account or internet.


It offers three study modes: Flashcards for passive review, Learn for typed-answer practice with instant feedback, and Test for auto-graded multiple choice.


Your progress is tracked locally, including daily accuracy, study streaks, and a live accuracy graph that grows with your streak.


Your study sets are stored locally, giving you the ability to switch between flashcard decks and upload new ones from your device.

How to use StudyForgeJS

Create or upload flashcards, learn by typing answers, and test yourself with auto-graded quizzes.

  • 🗂️ Upload a .txt flashcard deck (Q/A on alternating lines)
  • ✨ Or Forge a flashcard deck from scratch
  • 🏛️ Or select a deck from your library
  • 🧠 Study with Flashcards / Learn / Test modes
